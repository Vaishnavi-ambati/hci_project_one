In the era of technical advancement, mobile phones play a very significant role. Technology has made our life quite easier. Life without a mobile phone seems to be quite impossible these days. Precisely, we become handicapped without a phone in hand. Earlier it was only used for calling. But these days, everything is possible through a mobile phone. From sending a message to video calling, internet browsing, photography to video games, emailing and a lot more services can be availed through this handheld phone. The table provides information about various function types used by mobile phone users across 15 categories in five specific years 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, and 2020. Overall, it can be observed that the most frequent usage of the mobile phone was to make calls since it is one of the primary functions of a mobile phone. The “make calls” feature has been used by 100% of the users in all the years.

The growth in usage of “Send or Receive messages” features can be directly correlated to the growth of the usage of messaging apps like WhatsApp, Messenger, and Telegram. It is clear from the table that WhatsApp has established clear dominance with a usage percentage of 90% in 2020 as compared to the next market leader Instagram with a usage of 83%. Although WhatsApp was less popular in 2016 compared to Messenger with 25% usage in 2016 but WhatsApp usage has dramatically increased over the next years. Social applications are one of the most used applications in mobile phones. Although many applications exist in the market, Facebook and Instagram have established dominance. The usage of Facebook was in constant uprise from 45% in 2016 to 74% in 2019 but the usage has declined in 2020 to 72%. One of the reasons for the decline would be competition from other social applications like Instagram. Instagram’s usage has been increasing from 33% in 2016 to 83% in 2018.

The “File Transfer” feature has increased dramatically from 9% in 2016 to 69% in 2020. The reason for this might be the advent of USB technology. The “Online shopping” feature has also increased a lot from 40% in 2016 to 88% in 2020. Internet browsing has also been on the rise from 80% in 2016 to 96% in 2020. The least used feature is the Video recording feature with a usage percentage of 48% in 2020. With new features like “tap pay” and money transfer via mobile, the “Money transfer” feature usage has increased from 40% in 2016 to 82% in 2020. Because of the rapid increase in the PC gaming industry, there is no significant increase in mobile gaming. It has increased from 48% in 2016 to 62% in 2020.

In each of these years, the mobile use for calling was significantly higher than the other eight functions, with 100% usage levels in all five years. With the rapid increase in technology, usage of all the features and applications has increased dramatically over the past 5 years.
